This week was a bit of a roller coaster, lots of fun and not-so-fun things happened but I've come out of the other end feeling good and that's all that really matters.

# Absolute Cinema

[Yash](https://yashgarg.dev/) and I finally managed to grab tickets for an IMAX screening of [Project Hail Mary](https://www.imdb.com/title/tt12042730/) for Monday morning. Taking the half day off work felt worthwhile for how long we've had to wait for this, and by sheer coincidence I ran into [Rahul Gonsalves](https://www.rahulgonsalves.com/) at the theatre who also agreed that Monday mornings are the best show times :P

On Thursday I watched [Dhurandhar 2](https://www.imdb.com/title/tt39139925/) with Yash and [Swapnil](https://github.com/swapnilmadavi) which was honestly quite a letdown. The first movie was _obviously_ military and BJP propaganda, but the second one in so in-your-face about it that it really dampened my enjoyment of it. Since they were shot together, my complaints about the [first movie's CGI](/posts/weeknotes-week-51-2025/#:~:text=I%20watched%20Dhurandhar) also apply to this one. The shock factor of the incredibly gory movie constantly getting interrupted by god awful CGI that fully breaks your immersion makes for a very lame viewing experience.
