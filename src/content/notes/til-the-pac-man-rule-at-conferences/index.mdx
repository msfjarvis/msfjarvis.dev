I came across a Fediverse [post](https://fosstodon.org/@ericholscher/116579642732337477) today mentioning the Pac-Man rule being encouraged at PyCon and was pleasantly surprised by how such a small change can alter the dynamic of being in an intimidating conference setting.

Their [linked blog post](https://www.ericholscher.com/blog/2017/aug/2/pacman-rule-conferences/) lays it out pretty succinctly, but the core premise is just being slightly more intentional about how groups present themselves to other attendees. By leaving a gap in circle of people talking to each other, quite like Pac-Man's mouth, you change a closed group into one that invites people to join. So clever!
