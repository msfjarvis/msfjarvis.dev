While trying to share a [Grafana](https://grafana.com) dashboard today I saw an unfamiliar option: `Publish to snapshots.raintank.io` which led me to a small goose chase. Thankfully that option wasn't the result of some malicious plugin, it's just Grafana Labs' [old name](https://grafana.com/blog/we-are-now-grafana-labs/).
