# Personal Projects

Most of my week was spent mitigating the well documented constant barrage of AI scrapers doing a number on my [Git server](https://git.msfjarvis.dev). After a few missteps with [fail2ban](https://github.com/fail2ban/fail2ban) and Cloudflare's IP Access Rules, I've managed to land on the right rulesets on Cloudflare to keep the worst of it from hitting the server. According to Cloudflare's statistics, in the past week I've _blocked_ over 500K requests and even then I continued seeing problems until this morning when I finally got a comprehensive set of protections in place. I want to write a blog post about this since this was an annoying problem to deal with, but ultimately most of it is just "use Cloudflare" and I don't want it to come off as shilling. I am easily influenced, so reach out to express your interest if you want to read about it.

# Meta

The site is now running on Astro 7! Nothing user-facing should've changed, I've tried to not switch to any of the newer stuff like [Sätteri](https://satteri.bruits.org/) so I can evaluate it more comfortably. I did try out migrating to Sätteri, and was able to [get it done](https://github.com/msfjarvis/msfjarvis.dev/commit/2b1e6c22ae7b801e458a3846e20420d07736b8c9) but ultimately the UnifiedJS/Remark/Rehype ecosystem is much more mature and I don't currently feel the need for Sätteri's promised speedups.

I've had Atom, RSS and JSON feeds for most taxonomies but was shockingly missing one for posts, so that has now [been added](/posts/feed.atom) (thanks Yash for letting me know). After some discussion with Yash I've also decided to update the main feeds at `https://msfjarvis.dev/feed.[extension]` to start including my shorter [notes](/notes) as well, so expect a minor wave of spam in your feed readers. If you want to only follow a specific type of writing, you can provide its listing page to your feed reader (like `https://msfjarvis.dev/posts`, `https://msfjarvis.dev/weeknotes`, or `https://msfjarvis.dev/tags/NixOS/`) and the feed reader will pick up a specialized feed for it.

I had also forgotten to put back my analytics script when the site was moved to Astro, that's now back so I can continue ignoring the stats it's recording.
