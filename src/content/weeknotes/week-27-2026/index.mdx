# Personal

My health was finally good enough this week to return to the gym, felt nice to be back in the swing of things. Hopefully the weather does not screw me over and I can work up to my old schedule again.

## Reading

I've been ramping up my reading so I can hit my self imposed 24 books a year goal. I finished reading the 3rd book of the Chaos Walking trilogy, [Monsters of Men](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Monsters_of_Men). On [Amogh](https://kat.bio)'s recommendation, I've now started the [Mother of Learning](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Draft:Mother_of_Learning) series. I'm 200 pages in already, the story's great and I've found myself choosing to climb into bed with the Kobo at times where I'd usually pull up Steam or a terminal. I'm also making a change and [reading this on KOReader](https://masto-archive.msfjarvis.dev/@msfjarvis/116851965538384554) since I've had it installed for a while now and never really spent time with it to find what reading settings work for me.

# Work

Work was pretty quiet this week as we continued stumbling through one access problem after another trying to get new releases out for our Flutter SDK. Maybe next week 😔✊

In our weekly sync with the US team I got the chance to talk about some LLM-driven performance improvements I had made to our native SDKs, bringing our meeting join times down by ~20%. I still consider it to be somewhat slower than I'd like, so work on this front will continue.

We had a "minor" [incident](https://new.cloudflarestatus.com/incidents/4sfv79jntx9d) on Thursday night, our backend team spent multiple hours mitigating the fallout. Being on call in a company of this size is a scary prospect, and I'm always impressed with how incident response happens at Cloudflare.
