Welcome to the new website! It's powered by [Astro](https://astro.build) but still fully static to the highest extent possible, and performs impeccably on synthetic benchmarks. I was growing increasingly frustrated with both Hugo and Netlify, so this was a great opportunity to kill 2 birds with one stone and also move the site to Cloudflare.

I've painstakingly tried to keep all old links working even as I reorganized things, but please please let me know if you find anything out of place. There are still missing features due to technical and human limitations, which will be restored slowly over time:

- Automatically generated OpenGraph images
- GitHub Discussions powered comments (Not sure I want to bring this back TBH)
- The glorius post footer that let you share my writing to LinkedIn, boosting my street cred
- Incoming Webmentions viewer
- The build-time integration that I had on Netlify to queue pages for outgoing Webmentions
