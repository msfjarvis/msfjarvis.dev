## Reading

I've been very, very engrossed in the Mother of Learning series, so I've been trying to steal time for reading at pretty much every chance I get. Reading in bed has become my new favorite pastime, and also led to some ill-timed naps. All in all I'm reasonably confident about the 24 books a year reading goal now which was looking a little rough a couple of months ago.

The office was shockingly empty on Wednesday, so I stayed back for an extra 2 hours just to read in comfort and managed to [finish Mother of Learning: ARC 1](https://bookwyrm.social/user/msfjarvis/generatednote/11975508). No spoilers, but the ending was a crazy cliffhanger to leave the reader with. I can't imagine the anxiety of being a web series reader when this story was being written live and having to wait weeks to know what happened to the protagonist.

Thankfully, I can just flip over to the next book, which [I did](https://bookwyrm.social/user/msfjarvis/generatednote/11989593), and it's just as great.
